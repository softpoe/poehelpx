About Us

DataHelperX is a powerful automated trading bot designed to provide a seamless and efficient gaming experience. Whether you are a passionate player of Path of Exile or a beginner, DataHelperX will be your best companion, helping you enhance your gaming efficiency

Features

  • We provide a wider range of stash tab support compared to any other bot. With the exception of the Map tab, Divination Card tab, and Unique tab, we support all other tab types.
  • You can sell individual items or items in bulk from any supported stash tab.
  • Buy items from live searches directly from the official website, based on your criteria. You can also choose to reprice the item after buying, and set a maximum stock limit.
  • Purchase items directly from the official website's item search page, tailored to your criteria. Additionally, you have the option to adjust the item's price post-purchase and establish a maximum stock limit.
  • Procure bulk items from the official website's exchange search page, in line with your specifications. You also have the flexibility to revise the item's price post-purchase and define a maximum stock limit.
  • Never miss out on any valuable trades again due to appearing AFK. Our bot will ensure that you stay active and available.
  • Set prices for items or update prices for items in any supported tab without the need for any other pricing tool.
